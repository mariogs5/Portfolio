Alien Extraction

Alien Extraction is a videogame made in 4 months for the Project 3 subject on the third year of Videogame Design & Development degree.

The goal of this project was to emulate a professional video game studio, aiming to develop a game based on the guidelines provided by the product owners, who, in this case, were our teachers.

We had four months to deliver a complete and polished demo of the game, with progress submissions every two weeks. The product owners guidelines specified creating a DIABLO-like game set in the ALIEN universe.

I had the privilege of working within the Code Team, where I was in charge of these tasks:

- Audio Module Implementation: I integrated the Wwise audio engine into our custom game engine. This involved ensuring that sound effects and music responded accurately to in-game events, and optimizing audio performance for a seamless auditory experience.

- Main Character Implementation: I was responsible for developing and implementing the main character in the game. This included coding movement mechanics, combat abilities, and interaction systems. My work ensured that the main character was responsive, fluid, and engaging to control.
